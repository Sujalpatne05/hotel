## NEON FREE TIER LIMITS EXPLAINED

### 10 GB Storage
```
How much data is 10 GB?

For restaurants:
├─ 1 restaurant: ~100 MB
├─ 10 restaurants: ~1 GB
├─ 100 restaurants: ~10 GB ✅ (at limit)
└─ 1000 restaurants: ~100 GB ❌ (need upgrade)

Example data per restaurant:
├─ 1000 menu items: ~5 MB
├─ 10,000 orders: ~50 MB
├─ 100,000 inventory records: ~100 MB
└─ Total per restaurant: ~155 MB
```

### 100 Hours/Month Compute
```
How much is 100 hours?

100 hours = 3.3 hours per day
├─ 24/7 usage: ~4 hours/day ✅ (within limit)
├─ 8 hours/day: ~100 hours/month ✅ (at limit)
└─ 24 hours/day: ~720 hours/month ❌ (need upgrade)

For restaurants:
├─ 10 restaurants: ~10 hours/month ✅
├─ 100 restaurants: ~100 hours/month ✅ (at limit)
└─ 1000 restaurants: ~1000 hours/month ❌ (need upgrade)
```
