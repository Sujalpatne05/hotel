# Notifications - Quick Guide

## What Was Fixed
Notification buttons weren't opening pages. Now all three notifications work properly.

## How to Use

### Click the Bell Icon
- Located in top-right corner of dashboard
- Shows notification count (red dot)

### Available Notifications

1. **🔔 New order received!**
   - Click to open Orders page
   - View all new orders

2. **⚠️ Low stock warning!**
   - Click to open Inventory page
   - Check low stock items

3. **💰 High revenue milestone!**
   - Click to open Reports page
   - View revenue analytics

## Features

✅ All notifications work

✅ Smooth navigation
✅ Visual feedback

## Testing

### Test Each Notification
